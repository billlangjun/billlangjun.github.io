Welcome

Dr Jun LANG is a Research Scientist at Human Language Technology Department (HLT) of Institute for Infocomm Research (I2R), Singapore. He obtained the Ph.D. degree from Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT) in Jan. 2010. His major research interests include Natural Language Processing, Information Extraction, Machine Translation, and Machine Learning. Currently, he is working for Statistical Machine Translation, especially on Hierarchical Based SMT Research and System development.
